Make your own videos and create unforgettable memories!

Hello! This application was created in August/2025 as a final project for an Introduction to Computer Science course.

The goal of the application is to combine photos and videos into a beautiful and unique memory.

If you want to combine your photos/videos into a single video, you've come to the right place.

First, you will need to upload the photos/videos you want to combine. Then, you'll need to upload these selected files.

After uploading, you'll be able to process the video.

Currently, the default time for each photo is 3 seconds with a fade-in/fade-out effect and a resolution of 1920x1080.

Finally, don't forget to download the video. I hope you enjoy it!
